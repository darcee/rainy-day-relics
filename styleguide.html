Color Usage Guidelines

Backgrounds

Use Deep Navy (#1a2332) for main backgrounds and Stormy Blue (#2a3441) for content areas, cards, and sections.

Text

Antique Gold (#f4e8c8) for primary text and headings, Misty Blue (#8fa5b8) for secondary text and descriptions.

Interactive Elements

Teal Accent (#4a6b7c) for buttons, links, and hover states. Treasure Green (#3d5a4f) for success messages.

Accents

Use the gold sparingly for premium elements, teal for trustworthy actions, and green for positive feedback.
